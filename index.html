The golden thread

We are a poineering fashion start-up in Bangladesh dedicated to creating authentic Bengali tradional attire from waste materials. Our mission is to promote sustainable fashion and responsible waste management practices through innovative and beautiful designs.Waste Concern provides a comprehensive approach to waste management, including collection, segregation, recycling, and composting. They work with local communities, businesses, and municipalities to manage waste more effectively.
